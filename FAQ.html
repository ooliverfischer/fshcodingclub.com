Frequently Asked Questions

Can we use our chromebooks for projects?

Yes, but it isn't ideal for every project. Your projects will most likely be using some sort of program that is (sadly) blocked by the district. We recommend that you bring a personal computer to school. Macbooks, Linuxes, PS computers, and any other computer besides a chromebook/school enrolled computer will work. You can use a chromebook, but you will be excluded from multiple projects unless you can find a personal computer to use instead. It could be used from home too, as long as you take notes and paste them into the correct places at home.
