OUR GOAL(S)

Our goals for this club are simple. We want to provide the opportunty to code for anyone interested. That goes for anyone. Beginners, amateurs, and pros. We want beginners to have a safe place to learn, and we want people who already know how to code to have a community they can go to to share their code-related interests.

With the project bank, our goal is to motivate you guys to put your best efforts into your work in order to get your name remembered for many coding club years to come
